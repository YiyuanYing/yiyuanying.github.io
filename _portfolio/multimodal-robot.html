Project Overview

Supervisor: Professor Yi Jianjun | Collaborative Project with Shanghai Xiaoyuan Innovation Center

Duration: September 2024 - June 2025

Project Background

Multi-legged robots in aerospace missions, serving as "parasitic" intelligent agents of spacecraft, need to possess the capability of autonomous perception and decision-making in unknown environments after planetary surface landing, to construct a mission-oriented intelligent closed-loop system.

To achieve this goal, robot technology needs to meet the following requirements: First, establish a complete "perception-decision-planning-control" architecture that enables independent processing from environmental perception to final action; Second, possess multi-modal motion switching capabilities to adapt to different mission requirements and planetary surface environments; Finally, effectively adapt to and cope with complex and varied planetary surface terrain configurations, ensuring stable and reliable task execution under various harsh conditions.

Research Architecture

Multi-modal Humanoid Motion Perception and Interaction System

  • Multi-legged robot architecture design
  • Humanoid motion testing platform
  • Reinforcement learning serial control methods

This paper proposes a humanoid multi-legged robot control architecture: First, utilizing the multi-functional simulator Isaac Sim to build a humanoid motion testing platform, achieving interaction between simulation and real environments; Then, using multi-legged serial control methods and reinforcement learning to achieve robot teleoperation control; Finally, training and validating robot motion control algorithms.

Comprehensive Reinforcement Learning Intelligent Algorithm Design

Adopts a "Teacher-Student" training paradigm:

Teacher Policy: Constructs high-quality, smooth, and robust motion trajectories by collecting demonstration data in noise-free environments; Uses demonstration data to guide student policy training.

Student Policy: Improves student generalization and adaptability by aligning with teacher trajectories and combining actual noisy environment observations, while being able to adapt to complex environments.

Research Summary

  1. The research summarized multi-legged robot motion control technology under environmental perception and designed a reinforcement learning architecture that integrates six-degree-of-freedom robotic arms with multi-legged control systems.
  2. The research summarized the comparison between NVIDIA Isaac Sim simulation environment and experimental data in real environments, and combined NVIDIA Isaac Lab to build, conduct transmission, training, and Sim-to-Real transfer.
  3. The research summarized a control framework that fuses imitation learning and reinforcement learning, based on PPO and SAC algorithms, achieving stable gait and efficient motion control through "Teacher-Student" strategy and multi-legged strategy training.

Future Research

  1. Future research needs to continue studying multi-legged robot perception and decision-making technology with environmental interaction, and integrate it with reinforcement learning systems to address future complex tasks.
  2. Future research will continue to deeply investigate multi-legged robot remote operation control technology, exploring more precise and efficient remote operation methods to address future complex tasks.
  3. Future research will explore multi-legged robot motion control and planning under complex planetary surface terrain, and integrate it with multi-modal motion systems to address future complex tasks.

项目背景

航天任务中的多足机器人，作为航天器的“寄附式”智能体，在星表着陆后需具备在未知环境中进行自主感知与决策的能力，以构建一个任务导向的智能闭环系统。

为了实现这一目标，机器人技术需要满足以下要求：首先，建立一个完整的“感知—决策—规划—控制”架构，使其能够独立处理从环境感知到最终行动的全过程；其次，具备多模态运动切换能力，以适应不同任务需求和星表环境；最后，能够有效适应并应对复杂多变的星表地形构型，确保其在各种严苛条件下都能稳定可靠地执行任务。

研究架构

基于多模态的仿人运动感知与交互系统

  • 多足机器人架构设计
  • 仿人运动测试平台
  • 强化学习串行控制方法

本文提出了仿人多足机器人控制架构：首先，利用多功能仿真器 Isaac Sim 搭建仿人运动测试平台，实现仿真与真实环境交互；然后，利用多足串行控制方法与强化学习，实现机器人遥操作控制；最后，对机器人运动控制算法进行训练与验证。

教师策略：通过在无噪声环境中采集示教数据，构建高质量、平滑、鲁棒的运动轨迹；利用示教数据，用于指导学生策略训练。

学生策略：通过与教师轨迹进行对齐并结合实际噪声环境观测，提升学生的泛化性和适应性，同时能够适应复杂环境。

研究总结

  1. 研究总结了环境感知下的多足机器人运动控制技术，设计了融合六自由度机械臂与多足控制系统的强化学习架构。
  2. 研究总结了 NVIDIA Isaac Sim 仿真环境与实验数据真实环境对比，并结合 NVIDIA Isaac Lab 搭建并进行传输与训练以及SimReal迁移。
  3. 研究总结了一种融合模仿学习与强化学习的控制框架，基于PPO与SAC算法，通过“教师-学生”策略与多足策略进行训练，实现了稳定步态与高效运动控制。

后续研究

  1. 后续研究需要继续研究多足机器人与环境的感知与决策技术，并将其与强化学习系统相结合，以应对未来的复杂任务。
  2. 后续研究将继续深入研究多足机器人远程操作控制技术，探索更精确、更高效的远程操作方式，以应对未来的复杂任务。
  3. 后续研究将探索多足机器人在复杂星表地形下的运动控制与规划，并将其与多模态运动系统相结合，以应对未来的复杂任务。
